Eastbourne.Sussex. 7-1-19. NURSING SISTER H.N.STEVENSON struck off the strength to C.A.M.C.Casualty Coy.1-1-19.
CAPTAIN A.R.PERRY ceases to be on Command to P.P.C.R.C.Hospital,on proceeding to London to report for Embarkation Instructions,30-12-18. 
Three other ranks struck off the strength to C.A.M.C.Casualty Coy.5-1-19,as 21 date' casualties.
14-1-19. NURSING SISTER M.E.MAILLARD taken on the strength from C.A.M.C.Casualty Coy.8-1-19.
CAPTAIN W.F.KENNEY struck off the strength to Granville Canadian Special Hospital,9-1-19. 
MATRON B.J.WILLOUGHBY.RRC.OHK.proceeded on Command with the Matron-in-Chief to No.9 Canadian General Hospital,Rhyl,10-1-19 nad returned 14-1-19.
One Other Rank struck off the strength to C.A.M.C.Casualty Coy.13-1-19, on invaliding to Canada.
21-1-19. NURSING SISTER A.G.LUKE taken on the strength from Can. Spcl Hospital,Lenham,18-1-19.
